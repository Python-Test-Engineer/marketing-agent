# Content Calendar Planning

A content calendar transforms [[content_strategy_overview|Content Strategy Overview]] from theory into a production schedule. It ensures consistent publishing, cross-channel coordination, and no last-minute scrambles.

## Calendar Architecture

### Tiers of Planning

| Tier | Horizon | Contents |
|------|---------|----------|
| Strategic | Quarterly | Themes, campaigns, big launches |
| Editorial | Monthly | Topic list, format mix, assignments |
| Operational | Weekly | Final drafts, publish schedule, promotions |

## Content Mix Formula

Balance evergreen vs. timely content:

```
70% Evergreen (stays relevant 6–12+ months)
20% Timely (news-jacking, seasonal, trending)
10% Experimental (test new formats/channels)
```

## Weekly Cadence Example

| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|
| Monday | Finalize week's content, schedule posts |
| Tuesday | Long-form publish (blog/newsletter) |
| Wednesday | Social repurposing from Tuesday's piece |
| Thursday | Community engagement / UGC sharing |
| Friday | Analytics review → feed next week's plan |
