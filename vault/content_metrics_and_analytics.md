# Content Metrics and Analytics

Measurement closes the loop in [[Content Strategy Overview]]. Without it, you're optimizing by gut feel. With it, every piece of content teaches you something.

## Metrics Framework: The Ladder

```
Awareness (reach)
    ↓
Engagement (interest)
    ↓
Conversion (intent)
    ↓
Revenue (impact)
```

Map every KPI to one rung — vanity metrics live at the top, business metrics at the bottom.

## Key Metrics by Channel

### Blog / SEO → [[SEO and Content Marketing]]
| Metric | Target Signal |
|--------|--------------|
| Organic sessions | Growing MoM |
| Avg. time on page | >2 minutes |
| Scroll depth | >60% |
| Organic CTR | >3% from GSC |
| Backlinks acquired | Growing |

### Email → [[Email Marketing Strategy]]
| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| Open rate | 25–40% |
| Click-to-open rate (CTOR) | 10–20% |
| Unsubscribe rate | <0.3% |
| Revenue per email | Varies by product |

### Social → [[Social Media Strategy]]
| Metric | What It Signals |
|--------|----------------|
| Reach | Content is being discovered |
| Engagement rate | Content is resonating |
| Profile visits | Audience wants more |
| Link clicks | CTA is working |
| Follower growth rate | Brand momentum |

### Conversion
| Metric | Tool |
|--------|------|
| Lead magnet conversion rate | Landing page analytics |
| MQL from content | CRM attribution |
| Pipeline influenced by content | CRM / BI tool |
| CAC by content channel | Finance + marketing data |

## Attribution Models

| Model | How It Works | Best For |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| First touch | Credits first content interaction | Brand awareness campaigns |
| Last touch | Credits final interaction before conversion | Bottom-funnel content |
| Linear | Equal credit to all touchpoints | Understanding full journey |
| Time decay | More credit to recent touchpoints | Short sales cycles |

## Reporting Cadence

| Frequency | Report Type | Audience |
|-----------|------------|---------|
| Weekly | Channel performance snapshot | Content team |
| Monthly | Full funnel content report | Marketing leadership |
| Quarterly | Strategy review + pivot decisions | Executive team |

## Analytics Stack

- **Web:** Google Analytics 4, Plausible, or Fathom
- **SEO:** Google Search Console, Ahrefs/Semrush
- **Email:** Native ESP analytics (Klaviyo, ConvertKit, HubSpot)
- **Social:** Native insights + Sprout Social or Buffer Analytics
- **Attribution:** HubSpot, Triple Whale (e-commerce), or custom dashboards
