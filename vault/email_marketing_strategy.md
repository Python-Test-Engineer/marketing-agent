# Email Marketing Strategy

Email is your most valuable **owned channel** — no algorithm, no pay-to-play, direct line to your audience. It should be a core pillar in every [[Content Strategy Overview]].

## List Architecture

### Segmentation Dimensions
- **Persona** → from [[Audience Research and Personas]]
- **Funnel stage** → TOFU subscribers vs. active trial users vs. customers
- **Engagement tier** → active (opened in 90 days), cold, lapsed
- **Source** → how they joined (lead magnet, webinar, organic)

## Email Types

| Type | Purpose | Cadence |
|------|---------|---------|
| Newsletter | Thought leadership, content roundup | Weekly/biweekly |
| Welcome sequence | Onboarding new subscribers | Automated, 5–7 emails |
| Nurture sequence | Move MOFU leads to BOFU | Automated, triggered |
| Promotional | Launch, sale, event | As needed |
| Re-engagement | Win back cold subscribers | Quarterly |
| Transactional | Receipts, confirmations | Event-triggered |

## Newsletter Formula

```
Subject line: [Hook + curiosity/benefit]
Preview text: [Expand on subject line]

Intro (2–3 sentences): Hook and context
---
Section 1: Primary value piece (link to blog/resource)
Section 2: Secondary insight or tip
Section 3: Community/social proof moment
---
CTA: One clear action
P.S.: Second CTA or teaser for next issue
```

## Subject Line Frameworks

- **Curiosity gap:** "The mistake 90% of content teams make"
- **Specificity:** "3 email frameworks that doubled our open rate"
- **Personalization:** "[First name], here's your content audit checklist"
- **Urgency:** "Last chance: content strategy template (free)"
- **Contrarian:** "Why posting daily on LinkedIn is a waste of time"

## Deliverability Basics

- [ ] Authenticate domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- [ ] Warm up new sending domains slowly
- [ ] Clean list quarterly — remove bounces and unengaged
- [ ] Maintain <0.1% spam complaint rate
- [ ] Use double opt-in for new subscribers

## Key Metrics to Track

Track in [[Content Metrics and Analytics]]:
- **Open rate** (industry avg ~25–35%)
- **Click-to-open rate (CTOR)** — quality of content
- **Unsubscribe rate** — <0.3% per send is healthy
- **Revenue per email** (for e-commerce / product launches)

## Lead Magnet Ideas

Drive list growth with high-value offers:
- Templates and checklists (connect to [[Content Calendar Planning]])
- Mini-courses or email sequences
- Free audits or assessments
- Exclusive research or benchmark reports
